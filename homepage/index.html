About us
We originally created NCS back in 2015, after plug.dj shut down abruptly, effectively killing off entire communities. Some people stepped up to the plate, and created many alternative platforms. The most notable of which being musiqpad, a full replacement for plug.dj, as many others were, with one major difference. This was open source.
Why come back now?
NCS is being rewritten and revived now due to this years shutdown of plug.dj. I originally began rewritting it when plug's domain stopped functioning without additional information. All signs pointed to the site being abandoned, from a lack of updates and events, to the domain no longer actually pointing to a server. I didn't want to leave our community with nowhere to be, I didn't want it to dissapear, so I opened a musiqpad room and begain rewriting NCS from the ground up, to once again replace RCS.
